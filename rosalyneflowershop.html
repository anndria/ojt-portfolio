Project Overview

Rosalyne Flower Shop is an e-commerce platform that offers a wide range of floral arrangements, catering to the needs of modern customers. The shop aims to transform the floral industry by providing a flexible and scalable service designed to meet the evolving demands of its clientele. Its goal is to simplify the flower purchasing process while offering a versatile platform that adapts to the preferences and needs of diverse customers.
